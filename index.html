About us

Boboy's Fruits and Vegetables

Welcome to Boboy's Fruits and Vegetables, your online gateway to the freshest produce Catanduanes has to offer. We're a passionate team dedicated to bringing the vibrant flavors of our island directly to your kitchen. Our online market, conveniently located in District One, Gigmoto, Catanduanes, is your source for hand-selected fruits and vegetables, sourced directly from local farmers. We pride ourselves on offering the highest quality produce, ensuring every item is ripe, flavorful, and bursting with freshness.

At Boboy's, we believe in more than just delivering delicious food. We're committed to supporting our local community and promoting sustainable agriculture practices. By choosing Boboy's, you're not only enjoying the freshest produce, but also contributing to a thriving local economy and a healthier environment.
